FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What should I bring for my first appointment?

Please bring your ID proof, any previous eye examination records, current glasses or contact lens prescription, list of medications you're taking, and your insurance card if applicable.

Is LASIK surgery safe?

Yes, LASIK is one of the safest and most commonly performed surgeries. At Deepjyoti Eye Hospital, Dr. Jyoti uses the latest bladeless LASIK technology with a success rate of over 99%.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers and offer cashless facility. Please contact our billing department or bring your insurance card during your visit.

What is the recovery time after cataract surgery?

Modern cataract surgery is a quick procedure with fast recovery. Most patients notice improved vision within 24-48 hours. Complete healing typically takes 4-6 weeks.

How often should I have an eye exam?

We recommend annual eye exams for adults. Children should have their first exam at 6 months, then at 3 years, before starting school, and every 2 years thereafter.

Do you offer emergency eye care services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency eye care services. If you experience sudden vision loss, eye injury, or severe eye pain, please call our emergency helpline immediately.
